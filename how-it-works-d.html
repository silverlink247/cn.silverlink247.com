How does it work

Using the SilverLink pendant

In case you face an emergency situation such as a fall and need immediate assistance, SilverLink’s one touch pendant will allow you to summon help within minutes by alerting family members or concerned authorities, such as emergency response services. SilverLink’s emergency pendant is lightweight and easy to wear. It has one button on its surface that when pushed sends out an alert signal.
